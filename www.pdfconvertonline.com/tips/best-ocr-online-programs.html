Best OCR Online Programs

OCR has been made available online as a service (WebOCR), in a cloud computing environment, and in mobile applications like real-time translation of foreign-language signs on a smartphone.

Best Choice: Free-OCR

Free-OCR.com is a free online OCR program. You can use this service to extract text from any image you supply. This service is free, no registration necessary. It does not need your email address. Just upload your image files. Free-OCR takes either a JPG, GIF, TIFF BMP or PDF (only first page).

The only restriction is that the images must not be larger than 2MB, no wider or higher than 5000 pixels and there is a limit of 10 image uploads per hour.
