PDFConvert for Android

Do you have all kinds of document files such as PDF, Word, Epub or MOBI in your phone and you don't know how to open them? If so, you need a converter app to convert all kinds of documents to a format that your phone can recognize.

PDFConvert for Android is a cloud PDF converter app to easily create PDF from documents/images or convert PDF to documents/images on cloud server.

PDFConvert for Android can also split PDF, join PDF, rotate PDF, extract PDF, add or remove watermark, passwords and restrictions to PDF files.

First, you choose document, image or PDF files on your Android phone, then tap the "Convert Now" button to upload your files to cloud server, the server will return output files for you to download when it finishes conversion.

What's New: Added support for more formats such as EPUB, PPTX or XPS etc. Added PDF compressor. Prevent duplicated file conversion.

Notice: PDFConvert for Android works on Android 2.3 and later versions. It needs internet connection to run.
