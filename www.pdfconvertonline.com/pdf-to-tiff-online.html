Render DPI: DPI refers to the physical dot density of an image when it is reproduced as a real physical entity, for example printed onto paper, displayed on a monitor, or in this case rendered to image. TIFF Image Format: TIFF (Tagged Image File Format) is a computer file format for storing raster graphics images, popular among graphic artists, the publishing industry, and both amateur and professional photographers in general. The TIFF format is widely supported by image-manipulation applications, by publishing and page layout applications, and by scanning, faxing, word processing, optical character recognition and other applications.
